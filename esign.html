E-Sign Disclosure & Consent

Midly Labs Inc.  ·  Effective March 20, 2026
Applies to: Midly CLM (app.midly.ai)

Overview

By using Midly CLM to execute, sign, or send documents for signature, you consent to conduct business electronically and to use electronic signatures and electronic records in place of handwritten signatures and paper documents. Please read this disclosure carefully before proceeding.

This E-Sign Disclosure and Consent ("Disclosure") is provided in accordance with the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (E-SIGN Act, 15 U.S.C. § 7001 et seq.) and applicable state electronic signature laws. It governs your use of electronic signatures and your receipt of electronic records through Midly CLM, operated by Midly Labs Inc.

Your consent is entirely voluntary. You may choose to withdraw your consent at any time as described below, though doing so may affect your ability to use certain features of Midly CLM.

Scope of Consent

By consenting, you agree that electronic signatures and electronic records may be used for all agreements, contracts, documents, notices, disclosures, and communications between you and any counterparty executed through Midly CLM, including but not limited to:

  • Contracts and commercial agreements initiated or signed through the platform
  • Terms of service, end-user agreements, and account-related documents
  • Notices, amendments, and modifications to existing agreements
  • Any other documents you choose to send, sign, or manage through Midly CLM

This consent applies to your current and future use of Midly CLM for the purposes described above. It does not apply to documents or transactions conducted outside of the Midly CLM platform.

Hardware & Software Requirements

To access and retain electronic records provided through Midly CLM, you will need the following:

Browser A current version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version.
Internet A reliable internet connection capable of loading standard web applications.
Email A valid email address that you monitor regularly, used to receive notifications and document delivery.
PDF Viewer Software capable of opening and printing PDF files (e.g., Adobe Acrobat Reader, a built-in browser PDF viewer).
Storage Sufficient local storage to save electronic documents if you choose to download copies for your records.

If your hardware or software changes and you are no longer able to access electronic records, please contact us at legal@midly.ai so we can assist you.

Requesting Paper Copies

You have the right to receive a paper copy of any electronic record provided through Midly CLM. To request a paper copy, please contact us at legal@midly.ai with the subject line "Paper Copy Request" and identify the specific document(s) you require.

We will fulfill reasonable paper copy requests within a reasonable time. We reserve the right to charge a fee for paper copies where permitted by applicable law; if a fee applies, we will inform you before processing your request.

Your request for paper copies does not constitute a withdrawal of your consent to receive electronic records going forward.

Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw your consent to use electronic signatures and receive electronic records at any time. To withdraw consent, send a written notice to legal@midly.ai with the subject line "E-Sign Consent Withdrawal" and include your full name and the email address associated with your Midly CLM account.

Withdrawal of consent will be effective upon our receipt and confirmation of your notice. Please note:

  • Withdrawal of consent does not affect the validity of any electronic signatures or records created prior to the date of withdrawal.
  • If you withdraw consent, you may lose access to features of Midly CLM that rely on electronic signature functionality.
  • Documents already signed electronically remain valid and legally binding.

If you withdraw consent and later wish to re-consent, you may do so by contacting us at legal@midly.ai.

Updating Your Contact Information

It is your responsibility to keep your contact information — particularly your email address — current so that you continue to receive electronic records and notifications. To update your email address or other contact information associated with your Midly CLM account:

  • Log in to your account at app.midly.ai and update your profile settings; or
  • Contact us at legal@midly.ai with your updated information.

Midly Labs Inc. is not responsible for any failure to deliver electronic records resulting from inaccurate or outdated contact information.

Record Retention

Midly CLM retains electronic records, including signed documents and audit trails, for as long as your account is active and for a period thereafter as described in our Privacy Policy. You can access and download your signed documents at any time through your account dashboard.

We recommend that you save and store copies of all electronically signed documents for your own records. Midly Labs Inc. is not responsible for any loss of documents resulting from account closure, deletion, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control.
