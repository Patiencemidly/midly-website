How It Works

From intake to signed
in minutes, not days.

No more emailing Word docs back and forth. Midly handles the entire contract lifecycle in one place.

01
Create from a smart template
Sales or ops fills out a deal intake form. Midly auto-generates a contract using your legal-approved templates — no legal bottleneck required.
02
AI reviews for risk
Built-in AI flags missing clauses, unusual terms, and jurisdiction issues before anyone signs. Legal stays informed without being a bottleneck.
03
Collaborate and redline
Share a secure deal link with counterparties. Track changes, approve or reject redlines, and keep all comments in one place.
04
Sign and archive
eSignature built in. Signed contracts auto-archive with full metadata, searchable forever. No more digging through email.

Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Midly is built lean — powerful where it counts, invisible where it doesn't.

Smart Templates
Legal builds the guardrails. Sales fills out the form. Contracts generate in seconds — pre-approved, pre-formatted, ready to send.
AI Risk Insights
Catch missing indemnities, unusual liability caps, and jurisdiction gaps before they become problems. Inline, in plain English.
Deal Repository
Every contract, every version, every signature — searchable, filterable, and accessible to the right people at the right time.
Role-Based Permissions
Deal Owners edit. Reviewers redline. Legal approves. Everyone sees exactly what they need — and nothing they shouldn't.
Client Review Space
Send counterparties a secure link to review, redline, and approve — no PDF attachments, no version chaos, no back-and-forth email threads.
eSignature Built In
No third-party integrations needed. Send for signature, track status, and auto-archive completed contracts — all inside Midly.
