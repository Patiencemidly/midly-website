How It Works

From intake to signed
in minutes, not days.

No more emailing Word docs back and forth. Midly handles the entire contract lifecycle in one place.

01
Create from a smart template
Sales or ops fills out a deal intake form. Midly auto-generates a contract using your legal-approved templates (no legal bottleneck required).
02
AI reviews for risk
Built-in AI flags missing clauses, unusual terms, and jurisdiction issues before anyone signs. Legal stays informed without being a bottleneck.
03
Collaborate and redline
Share a secure deal link with your counterparty. They can review, comment, and redline. You accept or reject their changes and keep all discussion in one place.
04
Sign and archive
eSignature built in. Signed contracts auto-archive with full metadata, searchable forever. No more digging through email.
