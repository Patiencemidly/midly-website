The Problem

Sound familiar?

No consistent contract process across the team
Every deal owner does it differently. One sends a template, one uses last quarter's NDA, one just emails terms inline. No one knows what's been agreed.
Legal reviews everything — even standard terms — creating bottlenecks
Your legal team is talented and expensive. They shouldn't be reviewing the same MSA every other day. But without guardrails, they have to.
Deals stall between verbal yes and signed contract
Client says yes on the call. Then radio silence while you chase someone to send the contract, chase someone to review it, chase someone to sign it.
Nobody knows which contracts are pending, active, or expiring
Renewals get missed. Expirations go unnoticed. Nobody has a single view of contract status across the business.
The Solution

Legal owns the guardrails. Your team runs the deal.

Legal sets the templates, playbooks, and approval thresholds once
Legal defines the rules one time. From that point forward, the business runs deals within those guardrails — without pulling legal in every step.
Deal owners run the contract without pulling legal into every step
Generate from templates, send for counterparty review, get approved, execute — all from one deal room. Legal only sees what they need to see.
Full visibility into every deal's status across the team
One dashboard shows every deal — draft, pending review, out for signature, executed, expired. No more chasing status by Slack.
Counterparty redlines and reviews happen inside Midly — not over email
Counterparties access a clean deal room to review, comment, and redline. Every change is tracked. Nothing gets lost between inboxes.

Built for teams that close.

Up to 10 users. Playbooks. Approvals. Redlining. E-signature. All in one place.
