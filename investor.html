The Product

Midly manages the commercial deal workflow — from the moment both parties say yes to the moment the contract is signed.

For founders, sales directors, and operators closing deals without a legal team, that window is where momentum dies. Midly owns that window.

The platform handles deal tracking, counterparty collaboration, contract progression, and escalation to licensed counsel when needed — all in one place, built by the person who ran these workflows from the inside.

14-day free trial. No legal background required.

The Investment Signal
1

The distribution loop no one else has

Every deal closed on Midly routes the counterparty through a portal — a live product experience requiring no account, no setup, no sales call. Every customer generates the next warm prospect. CAC compounds down as the customer base grows up.

2

Built from the inside out

The founder built Midly because she lived the problem — not as an observer but as the person responsible for moving commercial deals across hundreds of negotiations. That firsthand experience is embedded in every product decision. Generic software teams building in this space are guessing at workflows she has run herself.

3

Deal intelligence that raises switching costs

Every contract, clause, counterparty, and outcome that runs through Midly becomes proprietary data. The product gets smarter with use. Customers' entire commercial history lives inside it. Switching cost grows with every deal closed.
