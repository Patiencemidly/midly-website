How Midly works

From handshake to signed —
without the chaos

Seven stages. Zero email threads. See exactly how Midly moves a deal from first word to final signature.

1 Stage one

Start with a
deal brief

Every deal begins with a conversation, not a template. You describe what you're building — the parties, the scope, the key commercial terms. Midly turns that into a structured deal brief in seconds, so nothing gets lost in translation between the handshake and the first draft.

2 Stage two

AI drafts the
contract for you

Based on your brief, Midly generates a complete first draft — MSA, SOW, NDA, whatever the deal needs. Your playbook, your fallback positions, your language baked in from the start. No blank page, no copy-paste from last quarter's deal.

3 Stage three

Loop in your
team to review

Tag colleagues directly in the contract. Finance checks the payment terms, legal reviews indemnification, ops confirms scope. Everyone sees the same version. Comments, approvals, and sign-offs all in one place — not split across six email threads.

4 Stage four

Send to the
counterparty

Share a secure link — no PDFs attached, no version confusion. The other side can review, comment, and propose changes directly in Midly. The redline arrives in your inbox, not a Word attachment buried in a reply-all thread.

5 Stage five

Negotiate
in the open

Track every change, every counter, every accepted clause in a clean audit trail. Midly flags the risk in proposed edits before you accept them. No surprises in the final version. No "I thought we agreed to remove that" moments.

6 Stage six

AI flags risk
before legal does

Midly's clause analysis catches the issues that slow deals down — uncapped indemnities, problematic governing law, IP overreach. Get plain-English summaries and suggested fallback language before it goes to outside counsel. Save the legal budget for the hard stuff.

7 Stage seven

Sign, file,
done

Collect legally binding e-signatures without leaving Midly. The executed contract is automatically filed in your repository, tagged, and searchable. Renewal dates tracked. Obligations logged. Next time someone asks "what did we agree to?" — the answer is one search away.
