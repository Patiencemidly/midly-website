About This Notice

This Notice to California Residents ("California Notice") is provided by Midly Labs Inc. ("Midly Labs," "we," "us") as a supplement to our Privacy Notice. It applies to California residents and describes your rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 ("CCPA") as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act of 2020 ("CPRA").

For purposes of this Notice, "personal information" has the meaning given in the CCPA and generally means information that identifies, relates to, describes, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or household.

We do not sell personal information. We do not sell, and have not sold in the preceding 12 months, any California consumer's personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration.

Right to Know

You have the right to request that we disclose to you, for the 12-month period preceding your request:

  • The categories of personal information we have collected about you
  • The categories of sources from which the personal information was collected
  • The business or commercial purpose for collecting, selling, or sharing personal information
  • The categories of third parties with whom we share personal information
  • The specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you

You may submit a Right to Know request up to twice in a 12-month period at no charge.

Right to Delete

You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions. We may deny a deletion request if retaining the information is necessary for us to:

  • Complete the transaction for which we collected the personal information
  • Detect security incidents or protect against malicious, deceptive, or illegal activity
  • Debug products to identify and repair errors
  • Exercise free speech or ensure another consumer's right to free speech
  • Comply with a legal obligation
  • Make other internal uses of the information that are compatible with the context in which you provided it

When we delete personal information, we will also direct our service providers to delete your personal information from their records, where required.

Right to Correct

You have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information that we maintain about you. Upon receipt of a verifiable request, we will use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the inaccurate information, taking into account the nature of the information and the purposes of processing.

You may correct most account information directly within the platform. For information you cannot update yourself, submit a request to legal@midly.ai.

Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Midly Labs Inc. does not sell California consumers' personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration, and has not done so in the preceding 12 months.

We also do not "share" personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under CPRA. Our analytics and advertising partners (Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity, Meta Pixel) operate under data processing agreements for specified purposes and do not receive personal information for their own marketing use.

If our practices change in a way that could constitute a "sale" or "sharing" under CCPA/CPRA, we will update this notice and provide a conspicuous "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on our website before any such sharing begins.

Right to Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights. We will not:

  • Deny you goods or services
  • Charge you different prices or rates for goods or services, including through granting discounts or other benefits or imposing penalties
  • Provide you a different level or quality of goods or services
  • Suggest that you may receive a different price or rate for goods or services or a different level or quality of goods or services
  • Retaliate against an employee, applicant, or independent contractor for exercising their rights

How to Submit a Request

To exercise your Right to Know, Right to Delete, Right to Correct, or Right to Opt-Out, you may submit a request by:

  • Email: legal@midly.ai with the subject line "California Privacy Request"

We will verify your identity before processing your request. We may ask you to confirm your email address, account details, or other information we have on file to verify that you are the consumer about whom we collected personal information. We will not require you to create an account with us to submit a request.

Response timeline. We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 10 business days and respond to the substance of your request within 45 calendar days of receipt. If we need additional time (up to an additional 45 days), we will notify you of the reason and the extended timeline.

We will provide our response in a portable, readily usable format. If you request specific pieces of personal information, we will deliver that information electronically via secure means.

Authorized Agent

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a CCPA/CPRA request on your behalf. To use an authorized agent, you must:

  • Provide the authorized agent written permission signed by you to submit the request
  • Verify your own identity directly with us

We may deny a request from an agent who does not provide proof of authorization. We will not hold it against you if we deny a request submitted by an agent who did not submit adequate proof of authorization.

Authorized agents may submit requests by emailing legal@midly.ai with proof of authorization attached.

California Shine the Light

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 ("Shine the Light") permits California residents who have an established business relationship with us to request information about the categories of personal information we have shared with third parties for their direct marketing purposes in the preceding calendar year.

We do not share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. Therefore, we do not maintain a list of such disclosures subject to Shine the Light. If you have questions about our practices, you may contact us at legal@midly.ai.
